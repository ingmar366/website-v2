About me

My jounery started in Bergen in the north of netherlands where I could enjoy the beautifull nature and the sea. After finishing highschool I went to The Hague university of applied siences to start studying 'Human Kinetic Technology'. In this multidisiplinary study with subjects like fysiology, atanomy, product design, advanced mathematics, I discoverd programming. This I have applied also in my final project where I have designed a module that could function as a sort of activity tracker for rollator users.

After university I took some time of to travel and see the world. As almost everyone at that time (2020) plans changed quite a bit, this because of corona virus. I did manage to travel but this was a bit more limited to spain. After working in countless simpeler jobs I discoverd that I would like to do something with my head again. At this time I discovered programming again and with some advice of friends I started to learn Javascript. In 5 months on tenerife i dedicated my self to learning and finishing the course on udemy (The Complete JavaScript Course 2022: From Zero to Expert). And now after this its time to challenge my self and try to push my self towards a professional developer. Lets see where this jounery is going to take me!

Next to ofcourse programming I, which is one of my hobbies I ofcourse also like to do other things; one of these is ofcourse hanging around watching series and the occasional gaming but thats only natural if your a bit of a geek like me. Also I enjoy going into nature and calm my mind, this go's from hicking to camping and traveling in a camper. With growing up sports haver been a big part of my life; this started with ice speed skating, surfing and later started with race cyling.
